About Us
MyPlants
MyPlants is a family-owned and operated company dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality indoor plants and gardening supplies. Our passion for plants began over a decade ago, when our founder, Badreddine, started growing herbs in his apartment. Over time, Badreddine's hobby turned into a full-fledged business, and he began selling his plants at local farmers' markets.

Today, MyPlants has grown into a thriving business with a team of skilled horticulturists who are passionate about bringing the beauty of nature indoors. We pride ourselves on sourcing the healthiest and most vibrant plants from local and international growers, and we use sustainable practices to reduce our environmental impact.

At MyPlants, we believe that plants have the power to improve our well-being and bring life to any space. Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your living room or a small succulent for your desk, we have the perfect plants for you. Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to answer your questions and help you choose the perfect plants for your needs.s
